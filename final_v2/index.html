W209 Final Project - What is My Market?

Knowing who you are selling a product to is hard! While lots of money goes into understanding customers, very little goes into understanding where they live and deriving information about what the whole market is for a given product. This semester we've worked on addressing these challenges through a creative data visualization to focus on providing a whole market picture across a variety of metrics.

Intended Audience

We've designed this tool with marketers and strategy professionals in mind, with a particular focus on providing contextual information about what the market distribution is, where it is located geographically, and the high and low points.

Using the Tool

To start using the tool, simply select one of the buttons on our top navigation bar to view the desired metric. You'll find three charts and a short narractive on each page. You can highlight and pan the geographic map as needed to extract details, view the overall distribution of values for the states.
